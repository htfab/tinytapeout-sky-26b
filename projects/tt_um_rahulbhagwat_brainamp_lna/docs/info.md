## How it works

This is  a low noise amplifier to amplify EEG waves (signals) generated by your brain, removing AC coupled noise and not adding too much noise of it's own.

## How to test

Connect an electrode (or a 100 milli-amp sine wave to EEG_IN).
Connect a similar less noisy wave to EEG_REF, and you should see an amplified output for low Hz (1-100) frequencies.

## External hardware

If you actually want to attach this to your head, might need silver coated dry EEG electrodes.
